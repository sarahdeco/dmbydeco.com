About

As someone who genuinely believes that great work requires a love for what you do, I am fortunate to have discovered a career path that aligns with my artistic passion.

What is D.M.?

I founded D.M. by Deco in 2020 as a creative studio specializing in graphic design and other marketing materials. "D.M." stands for "Deco Made," in other words, it was made by me. "D.M." is also a purposefully open-ended term, it can stand for "Digital Media," "Dynamic Messaging," or "Design and Marketing."

Who is Deco?

Originally from Fairfield County, Connecticut, I spent my formative years at Walnut Hill School for the Arts in Natick, Massachusetts. Later, I furthered my education at the University of Miami in Coral Gables, Florida, earning a dual Bachelor of Art in Anthropology and Studio Art with a Minor in Africana Studies.

Why Choose D.M.?

I naturally gravitate towards activities that enable me to tap into my intuitive creativity. Lately, I have blended my vast understanding of human behavior with my passion for the visual arts, steering my career toward marketing communication and design. I am passionate about crafting innovative projects that promote inclusive values.
